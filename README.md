# Computing and AI for All Curriculum Website

This website shares free K–8 curriculum pathways for teaching computing, Scratch coding, AI literacy, computational thinking, and science inquiry. The materials are designed for real classrooms and for educators and learners with a range of backgrounds.

Use the homepage to explore available curriculum pathways, including ACT 1, ACT 2, ACT 3, Computing+AI, AI Literacy, Science Inquiry Studio, and Scratch Basics. Each pathway page includes unit overviews, lesson materials, teacher resources, and links to supporting documents.

The site also includes information about the project, partner districts, research team, and contact information for questions or feedback.
