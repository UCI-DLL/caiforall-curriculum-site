Coding+AI

Grades 6–8 lessons that develop AI literacy, computational thinking, and coding skills through AI-assisted and agentic coding.

Unit 0: Prerequisites

This manual is your guide to our middle/high school AI coding curriculum, featuring CreatiCode—an AI-enhanced version of MIT Scratch. Inside, you’ll find summaries of 2 documents to quickly grasp key concepts and tools, preparing you to bring AI and creativity into your classroom. Explore the full tutorials at your own pace to confidently inspire your students. Let’s get started!
